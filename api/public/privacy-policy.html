Privacy Policy for Bill Splitter App

1. Introduction ▼

This privacy policy explains how Bill Splitter ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("app").

2. Information We Collect ▼

Our app collects the following information:

  • Images of restaurant receipts captured through your device's camera
  • Images uploaded from your device's storage
3. How We Use Your Information ▼

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of processing restaurant receipts to facilitate bill splitting. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently by our app or on our servers.

4. Data Processing and AI ▼
  • Images are sent to Google's Gemini AI model for processing with vision support.
  • This processing occurs in real-time, and images are not retained after processing.
  • We do not have access to or store the processed images.
5. Data Storage and Security ▼
  • We do not store any images or personal data from our users.
  • All image processing is done using temporary buffers and the data is immediately discarded after use.
6. Third-Party Services ▼

Our app uses Google's Gemini AI service for image processing. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

7. Your Rights ▼

As we do not store personal data, there is no personal information for users to access, modify, or delete. The app's functionality is provided without the need for user accounts or stored personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy ▼

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us ▼
